Registration

At least one author of each accepted paper must register at full fee before 10th November, 2020. Papers without at least one registered author will not be included in the final program for presentation and ieee explore publication.

Student registration is not eligible for the paper publication and inclusion in conference program/proceedings.

Each full fee registration is entitled to present/publish up to a maximum of two (2) manuscripts. The manuscripts may contain a maximum of six pages. Two (2) extra pages are permissible with an additional fee to be paid at the time of registration. The charges for per additional page are INR 500 (+ 18% GST) for Indian delegate and USD 20 (+ 18% GST) for Foreign delegate. The payment gateway charges/Processing fees(4%) if any,will be borne by the authors.

Registration Guidelines:

  • Over length manuscript in excess of six pages are allowed after paying extra page charges.
  • IEEE members are requested to provide the IEEE membership number during the online registration process if registering under IEEE category. Authors claiming subsidized registration fees as IEEE members need to provide the valid IEEE membership number.
  • Students need to upload valid student ID card if registering under student category.
  • Please keep a copy of the transaction ID, generated by the payment gateway as well as by your bank. You may need these later if you need to track your payment/registration.
  • The registration fee paid is non-refundable.

Registration Steps:

Step 1.
Choose your registration as per category. Buy one registration for registering one paper. However, in case of each full registration (not student registration), it is allowed to register up to a maximum of two (2) manuscripts. Buy additional registrations if required.

Likewise, extra pages above page limit can be bought. Select the number of pages very carefully (select one from drop-down menu for one additional page and select two in case of two additional page). Please note here that if you are registering for total two manuscripts in single full registration, then you need to pay the extra page charges for total extra pages of two papers (this may vary from 1 to 4 in case of registration of two papers under full registration).

Step 2.
Enter buyer details (Buyer name, organization). Anyone can buy the registration ticket. Please note that the invoice will be given in the name of the buyer. In case, buyer does not have GST details, uncheck the Do you want Tax Invoice? and proceed.
Step 3.
Enter attendee details. The first attendee is considered as the registering author. Please take extreme care to provide Correct paper ID in each of the attendee details sheet.
