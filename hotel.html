ACCOMMODATION

A limited number of paid accommodation is available for students in Institute Hostel and shall be available on “First Come First Serve” basis. Applicants shall have to deposit the complete amount expected for their stay at Hostel office on arrival.

HOTELS NEAR MNIT JAIPUR

Those applicants who wish to stay outside campus may find many budget and better hotels in near vicinity of the campus within walking distance.

BUDGET HOTELS

  • The Red Fox Hotel, Jaipur,  is a budget hotel right opposite Malaviya National Institute of Technology (MNIT). It is also conveniently close to both domestic and international airport.

  • The Fern is a 3.5 star Hotel, situated on Tonk Road, is 3 kms from Malaviya National Institute of Technology (MNIT), Conference Venue. The hotel is located at a close proximity to Jaipur International airport, Railway Station, and Bus stand.

  • Hotel Pearl Palace, is a Heritage property, and caters to the younger backpacker budget-minded crowd. Very prominently located opposite World Trade Park and 1.5 kms from the Airport and is 2 kms from Malaviya National Institute of Technology (MNIT), Conference Venue.

  • Bella Vista This is a modern and elegantly designed budget hotel and is situated just a few blocks from JLN Marg, the commercial hotspot for business and entertainment in Jaipur. Very prominently located opposite World Trade Park and 2.5 kms from the Airport and is 1.5 kms from Malaviya National Institute of Technology (MNIT), Conference Venue.

  • Hotel Landmark It is located about 3.5 kilometres from Jaipur International Airport and 10 kilomtres from the main Railway Station. It is 1.5 kms from Malaviya National Institute of Technology (MNIT), Conference Venue .

OYO Rooms  are also available in nearby locations of MNIT Jaipur.


OTHER HOTELS

  • The Lalit, Jaipur is a 5 star deluxe hotel, minutes away from the international airport and major cultural attractions. Inspired by local architecture, the hotel is a fine blend of traditional & contemporary style reflecting rich cultural heritage of Rajasthan through its grand haveli architecture and resplendent interiors

  • Marriott Hotel Jaipur is a 5 star delux hotel and welcomes guests with refined elegance, contemporary amenities, and a prime location near Jaipur International Airport. The advantage is it is 10 minutes from the Airport. Has a modern look.

  • The Clarks Amer, Jaipur is a 5 star delux hotel, situated in a green residential area, in the heart of the city. Ideal for travelers. Jaipur Airport is just 2.5 Kms. The advantage is it is 5 minutes from the Airport. Very prominently located on the same road as the conference venue, 10 minutes from the Venue. More than 25 years in Business.

  • Radisson Blu Jaipur is a 5 star retreat, situated on Tonk Road, is 3 kms from Malaviya National Institute of Technology (MNIT), Conference Venue. Conveniently situated just two kilometers from Jaipur International Airport (JAI) and within walking distance of World Trade Park, one of the area’s leading corporate and shopping destinations.

  • The Royal Orchid is a 5 star hotel, Located at Tonk Road, 3 kms from Malaviya National Institute of Technology (MNIT), Conference Venue. The hotel is located at a close proximity to Jaipur International airport, Railway Station, and Bus stand.
